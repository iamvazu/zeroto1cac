## 📍 4. Local SEO Integration (Safe scaling)

To combine the local credibility of both **Bay Area** and **Boston** securely without keyword stuffing, incorporate them into the **Founder backstory** or **Office locations** section:

> **Example Placement (Footer or Promise Subtext):**
> *"Founded by experts from the **Silicon Valley (Bay Area)** tech ecosystem and informed by advanced curricula design from **Boston's** academic hub. We understand exactly what elite tech clusters demand."*

This positions the locations as **Heritage/Credibility tags** instead of SPAM keywords, which Google highly favors in their E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) guidelines.
